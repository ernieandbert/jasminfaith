JUST FOR THE NIGHT

Created for 3603QCA, Just for the Night was my final year project in which I was tasked with developing a creative business idea and seeing it come to fruition. The final product Just for the Night is a business that specialises in temporary pop-culture tattoos.

Taken from popular culture, each of the designs reflect a well liked quote with the tattoos individually wrapped. The packaging itself is made from 100% recycled paper with plastic kept to a minimum so as to ensure a sustainable and environmentally friendly design.

MUCHACHOS

The following logo was designed for the Muchachos, the name of a ficticious bike gang that my friends at work created. Named at randomly by a fellow co-worker, they wanted the logo to follow the standard look and feel of a bikie gang, but have hints of Mexican throughout the design.

In the end, it was decided to deck the skull out with a sombrero and place chillies under the skull in place of the crossed bones. If you look closely in the white logo on the wallpaper, you'll also find a taco hidden in the letters of the C and the H.

SAVE THE REEF

Created for the 2612QCA Typography for Screen Design final submission, the assignment required students in pairs of two to create what could be a viral media campagign for a cause they supported with heavy emphasis on typography. Focussing around the Great Barrier Reef, the campaign used an online web-presence, print media, merchandise and live events amoungst other things to help raise public awareness.
