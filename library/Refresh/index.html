TREATMENTS

Refresh Salons have designed a range of facials delivering targeted results for a range of skin types and concerns. In November 2012, Refresh Salons was launched - a lifestyle salon created for women who not want to look good, but feel great too. Refresh Salons is a representation of what creates true health and beauty both internally and externally. Take advantage of our full range of services and programs designed to replenish, rejuvenate and reinvent your mind body and spirit.

RAW
FOOD

You are what you eat! So it makes sense to consume food that is nutrient dense, alkalising, healing and tasty. Refresh raw food classes are designed to propel you to next level of wellness & vitality regardless of where you are at in life at the moment. We believe the best way to learn about food is to have a tactile experience. Hands memorise way better than our brains and that is why we run monthly raw food classes. We guarantee that you will complete the course feeling elated, inspired, empowered and equipped with wild knowledge and experience from head to toe.
